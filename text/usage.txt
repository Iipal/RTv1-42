Usage:
    [W A S D] - Move camera Up Left Down & Right.
    [Q E] - Dec\Increase camera Z-position.
    [LCtrl LShift] - Dec\Increase camera speed.
    [Z] - Toggle FPS\ms rendering info.
    [ESC] - exit.

    [X] - Switch movements binds to control light.
        After press [X] binds are:
        [W A S D] - Move all lights origin Up Left Down & Right.
        [Q E] - Dec\Increase all light sintensity.

        [C] - Switch to control lights Z-position.
        After press [C] binds are:
            [Q E] - Dec\Increase all lights Z-position.
