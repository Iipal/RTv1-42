Flags usage:
 ./RTv1 [flags-params] scenes/scene_file.rtv1
 -h, --help: print this message
 -dbg, --debug: activate switcher binds mode. Default: not enable.
 -vps, --viewportScale: integer param 1-10. Default: 1.
 -al, --ambientLight: integer param 1-25. Default: 100.
 -ftc, --fpsTextColor: hex param 0x1-0xffffff. Default: 0x80ff00.
 -frt, --fpsRefreshTimer: how often will refresh FPS counter. Default: 25(in ms).
 -ncl, --noCalcLight: disable calc all light origins. Default: not enable.
 -t, --textured: enable textured rendering. Default: not enable.
 -rli, --randomLightsIntense: enable randomatic lights intense. Default: not enable.
 -pu, --printUsage: print -dbg mode usage. Default: not enable.
