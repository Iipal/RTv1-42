Flags usage:
 ./RTv1 [flags-params] scenes/scene_file.rtv1
 -h, --help: print this message
 -dbg, --debug: activate switcher binds mode. Default: not enable.
 -vps, --viewport-scale: integer param 1-10. Default: 1.
 -sb, --shadow-bright: integer param 1-10. Default: 10.
 -ftc, --fps-text-color: hex param 0x1-0xffffff. Default: 0x80ff00.
 -frt, --fps-refresh-timer: how often will refresh FPS counter. Default: 25(in ms).
 -ncl, --no-calc-light: disable calc all light origins. Default: not enable.
 -t, --textured: enable textured rendering. Default: not enable.
 -pu, --print-usage: print -dbg mode usage. Default: not enable.
